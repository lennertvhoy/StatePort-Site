Here is the StatePort preview. It gives an AI project a place to live outside
the chat. When you come back, you can see the project, what you were doing, and
what needs your attention.

This is the conversation screen. You can ask for help, but the conversation is
only one part of the project. Your files and decisions do not disappear inside
a long chat.

This screen shows the project information. It helps you see what is ready, what
is still being checked, and what is only a test. That makes it easier to know
what you can trust.

And this is the mobile view. The same project stays readable on a small screen.

This is a local preview made on my computer. You cannot download the software
yet. The point of StatePort is simple: help you pick up AI work again without
losing the work itself.

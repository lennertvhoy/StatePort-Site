StatePort starts with your work, not a blank chat. You open a named project
and see what is happening, what needs attention, and what you did last time.

The important files and decisions stay close to the project. In this preview,
you can see what is ready and what is still being checked.

Now ask a short question. The assistant helps move the work forward, and the
reply stays with the project.

If you need technical tools, they are there, but they do not take over the
whole screen. If something is unavailable, the product shows that clearly.

You can also see which version of the project you are looking at. A test
version is not quietly presented as a finished release.

This is a local preview, recorded on one computer. It shows the shape of the
experience. It is not a public download or a production promise.

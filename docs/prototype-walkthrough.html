What you will see

  1. Your work in one place. Open a named project instead of starting from a blank chat.
  2. Files and decisions stay close. See the information behind the work while you use it.
  3. Ask one question. Get help without losing the rest of the project.
  4. More tools when you need them. Extra technical controls stay out of the way until they are useful.
  5. Know what is ready. See the difference between something being tried, checked, and released.
  6. Use it on mobile. The same project remains easy to understand on a small screen.

What this video does not show

This is a local preview, not a download or product release. The proof and roadmap page explains what still needs to happen.

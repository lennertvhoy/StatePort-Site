When it is ready

A release earns its place here with material people can inspect and use.

  • A public source boundary and license decision.
  • A versioned source and release notes.
  • Published artifacts or installers with checksums where appropriate.
  • A Docker-first installation path for the stated environment.
  • A clear record of what has been checked and accepted.

Why a ledger?

Release details deserve the same care as the work itself. One page makes it easier to see what is ready and what is still being prepared.
